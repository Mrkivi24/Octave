About This Site and a guide!

Welcome to Our Chatroom and Forum Application

About the Application

This application is a platform where users can engage in live chats, participate in forums, and send private messages to other users. It’s designed to provide a seamless and interactive experience for all users.

How to Get Started

  1. Sign Up: Create an account by providing a username and password on the Sign Up page.
  2. Log In: Use your credentials to log in on the Log In page.

Features and Usage Guide

1. Live Chatroom

The chatroom allows all users to participate in live discussions. Messages sent in the chatroom are visible to everyone.

  • Simply type your message in the input box and press Enter to send.
  • Messages update in real-time; no need to refresh the page.

2. Forums

Forums provide a platform to start discussions on specific topics and allow users to reply with comments.

  • Create a Forum Post: Navigate to the Forums page and click on "New Post". Add a title and content to start a discussion.
  • Reply to Posts: Click on a forum post to view its details and add your comment in the provided form.

3. Direct Messaging

You can send private messages to other users directly.

  • Start a Conversation: In the chatroom, right-click on a user’s name and select "Direct Message".
  • Real-Time Updates: Messages in direct conversations are updated live, just like the chatroom.

4. Mute Users

If another user is being disruptive or offensive, you can mute them to stop seeing their messages.

  • Mute a User: In the chatroom, right-click on the user’s name and select "Mute User".
  • Their messages will no longer appear for you, but other users can still see them.

5. Automatic Chat Cleanup

To ensure the chatroom remains clutter-free, all messages older than 24 hours are automatically deleted. Forum posts and comments are not affected.

FAQs

What happens if I forget my password?
Currently, password recovery is not supported. Please contact the admin for assistance.
Are my private messages secure?
Yes, private messages are only visible to you and the recipient.
Why can’t I see messages from a certain user?
You may have muted the user. To unmute them, contact the admin for help.
